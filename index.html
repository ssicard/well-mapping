Louisiana Oil and Gas Portal

This website is intended to provide general information about the oil and gas resources in Louisiana. It is designed to be an educational tool and should not be used for any other purposes.

The data is sourced form the oil and gas production database maintained by the Louisiana Department of Natural Resources. Data quantity may vary and require further work to achieve desired accuracy. We are not liable for data accuracy or use of these data.
